Analyzing Homelessness Trends Across the US and Ireland

This project investigates the socioeconomic drivers of homelessness by manually sourcing and cleaning data for multiple influential factors from various websites and references across both the United States and Ireland. The study uses regression models such as Random Forest, Ridge, and LASSO, and evaluates variable impact through comparative modeling techniques between the two countries.

  • 🧭 Manual data sourcing for both US & Ireland from credible references
  • 📊 Feature selection and importance using ML models (Random Forest, LASSO)
  • 📈 Comparative modeling to analyze key drivers of homelessness
  • 🧮 Visual insights via EDA in Python and modeling in R
